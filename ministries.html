About ELI Asia

Evangelical Leadership Institute Asia (ELI Asia) seeks to enable Christian leaders to acquire theological and ministerial education equivalents to those offered in Bible colleges, without leaving their churches for a long time.

Advancing Ministerial Training

ELI Asia exists to serve and function as the theologoical education and training arm of the local church. It comes alongside churches to develop pastors and Christian leaders so they can effectively shepherd their congregations and equip them to make disciples for Christ.
