About

Our name says something about who we are

Union With Christ Community Church.

As believers we are one with Christ through the indwelling of the Holy Spirit, and we experience intimate union with God through his son Jesus Christ. This union with Christ is what forms us into the body of Christ – His church.

Community clearly identifies the sphere of our influence. As members of one body, we seek to preserve the unity of the Spirit in the bond of peace. As a light in our community, we seek to make Christ known through His love.

Church reminds us that we are not a club with earthly or temporal purposes. Our goal, directions, methods, standards and purpose are all defined and charted by God. We exist for the audience of One. As His church, we are continuing the ministry that Jesus started, which is to connect mankind with our amazing God. And we would love to connect with you.

Our Mission And Vision

Mission

UCCC exists to love God and man with a renewed mind and spirit and to magnify Christ and make him known.

Vision

UCCC’s vision is to reach our community with the message and love of God, one family at a time.

Distinctives

Inerrancy of the Bible

We believe the Bible to be the inspired and inerrant Word of God. It is the only source and standard for matters of faith and practice for the believer.

Salvation is by Grace Through Faith Alone

We believe that salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone, and to the glory of God alone and that the gospel call to faith presupposes that sinners must repent from their sins and yield to Christ’s authority.

Sovereignty of God in Salvation

We believe that GOD, before the foundation of the world, sovereignly elects in Christ those whom He graciously regenerates, saves, and sanctifies.

Eternal Security

We believe that all the redeemed once saved are kept by God’s power and are thus secure in Christ forever.

Cessation of Sign Gifts or Apostolic Gifts

We believe that miracles, tongues and vision are sign gifts to validate the gospel message and its messenger. And that the evidence of Scripture and history indicate that tongues ceased in the Apostolic age and that there are no more apostles nor New Testament prophets today.

Spiritual Equality of Man and Woman but with Different Roles

We believe that man and woman are equall spiritually, yet they have different intended roles both in the ministry and in the home.

Mission of Local Churches

We believe that the primary tasks of a local church are to make disciples by proclaiming the Gospel and to shepherd believers to spiritual maturity.

Important Role of a Local Church and Church Membership

We believe that every believer should be a member of and committed and accountable to a local church.

Plurality of Godly Leaders

We believe that Jesus Christ is the invisible head of our church and He is ruling through godly elders.

Expository Preaching as the means to Maturity

We believe that only expository preaching best accomplishes the call to proclaim the whole counsel of God. And it is the foundation of all our ministries.

Israel and Church Different and Distinct

We believe that the Church today does not replace the nation Israel. God will fulfill all His promises to the nation Israel.

Pre-Mil, Pre-Trib Return of Jesus Christ

We believe that the physical and literal return of Jesus Christ to the earth precedes the establishing of His physical kingdom here on earth.

Our Regular Pulpit Ministers

Pastor Nicky Joya

Nicky Joya is an ordained pastor and has been in the ministry for more than 30 years. He is currently the Executive Director of Evangelical Leadership Institute Asia (ELI Asia). His vision and passion is to equip pastors and workers for effective ministry especially in the rural provinces where the need for theological training is great. Since UCC Church’s establishment in 2012, Nicky has been involved in preaching during Sunday services, teaching Bible classes, and training its leaders. He is husband to Sally, father to Patrick, Paula and Pamela, and a grandfather of two boys.
