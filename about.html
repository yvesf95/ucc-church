Union with Christ Community Church was conceived and organized in answer to the call of a few old and new converts who were in search of a church that is Bible-based in doctrine and practice.

It all started when on August 31, 2012, Rev. Billy Arcayan, having waited upon the Lord and having convinced that it’s about time to leave, resigned as Senior Pastor of his church. Some leaders and members, who were discontent themselves and seeking for truth, praxis, guidance and spiritual growth, upon hearing Rev. Billy’s plan to resign, came with a request for him to start holding Bible studies at their residences as they looked for a new church to belong.

Initially, Rev. Billy agreed to their request and even gave several suggestions for good churches. But God in His supreme power intervened. With prayers, guidance and confirmation from several pastors that these leaders and members have seen the grace of God in his leadership, the first fellowship and prayer meeting was held at the house of Bro. Jesse and Sis. Cristina Young in Samat, Quezon City with 29 people attending.

The first Sunday Worship Service was held in Teriyaki Boy, Banawe Branch, Quezon City with 31 adult and 5 kids attending. The Worship Service continued for 12 months; and soon, the venue became too small to accommodate the church goers that the need for more space was called for. Hence, on the first Sunday of September, 2013, God provided for the much needed place at the 6th floor, Hemady Square at E. Rodriguez corner Dona Hemady St., Quezon City. By the grace of God, Hemady Square agreed to let UCC Church use the place for a year only for free.

Again, as a growing church, a few concerns surfaced but God turned them all into a blessing and a learning experience. Our prayer will always be for God to find Union with Christ Community Church faithful from start to the end!

We believe the Bible to be the inspired and inerrant Word of God. It is the only source and standard for matters of faith and practice for the believer.

We believe that salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone, and to the glory of God alone and that the gospel call to faith presupposes that sinners must repent from their sins and yield to Christ’s authority.

We believe that GOD, before the foundation of the world, sovereignly elects in Christ those whom He graciously regenerates, saves, and sanctifies.

We believe that all the redeemed once saved are kept by God’s power and are thus secure in Christ forever.

We believe that miracles, tongues and vision are sign gifts to validate the gospel message and its messenger. And that the evidence of Scripture and history indicate that tongues ceased in the Apostolic age and that there are no more apostles nor New Testament prophets today.

We believe that man and woman are equall spiritually, yet they have different intended roles both in the ministry and in the home.

We believe that the primary tasks of a local church are to make disciples by proclaiming the Gospel and to shepherd believers to spiritual maturity.

We believe that every believer should be a member of and committed and accountable to a local church.

We believe that Jesus Christ is the invisible head of our church and He is ruling through godly elders.

We believe that only expository preaching best accomplishes the call to proclaim the whole counsel of God. And it is the foundation of all our ministries.

We believe that the Church today does not replace the nation Israel. God will fulfill all His promises to the nation Israel.

We believe that the physical and literal return of Jesus Christ to the earth precedes the establishing of His physical kingdom here on earth.
